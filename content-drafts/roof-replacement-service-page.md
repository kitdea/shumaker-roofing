## Why Replace Your Roof?

Most roofs have a lifespan of 20–30 years. If yours is showing any of these signs, it may be time for a full replacement:

- **Shingles are curling, cracking, or missing** in multiple areas
- **Granule loss** is visible in your gutters after rain
- **Leaks or water stains** appear on your ceilings or attic
- **Sagging sections** or visible daylight through the roof deck
- **Your roof is 20+ years old** and repairs are becoming more frequent

A new roof isn't just a repair — it's an investment that protects your home, improves energy efficiency, and adds real value to your property.

## Materials We Install

### Asphalt shingles
The most popular choice for Maryland homeowners — affordable, reliable, and available in dozens of colors and profiles. We install leading brands including CertainTeed, GAF, and Owens Corning.

### Architectural (dimensional) shingles
A step up in durability and curb appeal. Architectural shingles are thicker, rated for higher winds, and designed to mimic the look of wood shake or slate at a fraction of the cost.

### Metal roofing
Standing seam and metal shingle systems are built to last a lifetime. Metal roofing reflects heat, holds up in heavy snow and ice, and requires almost no maintenance. Ideal for homeowners who want the last roof they'll ever need.

## FAQ Section

**How long does a roof replacement take?**  
Most residential replacements are completed in one to two days, depending on roof size and complexity. We'll give you a realistic timeline before we start.

**How much does roof replacement cost in Frederick, MD?**  
Cost varies based on roof size, pitch, material, and decking condition. Most residential replacements in the area range from $8,000 to $20,000+. We provide free, detailed estimates so you know exactly what to expect.

**Will my homeowner's insurance cover a roof replacement?**  
If your roof was damaged by storm, hail, or wind, your insurance policy may cover the replacement. We work with homeowners through the claims process and can document damage to support your claim.

**Do you offer financing?**  
Yes. We offer flexible financing options to fit a range of budgets. Ask us about current plans when you request your estimate.

**How long will my new roof last?**  
Asphalt shingles typically last 20–30 years. Architectural shingles are rated for 30+ years. Metal roofing can last 40–70 years or more with minimal maintenance.
